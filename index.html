Welcome to MASA!

Malaysian Student Association at University of Washington

Who we are

We are a close-knit group of Malaysian students at the University of Washington, Seattle, brought together by a shared heritage, culture, and a passion for supporting one another in our academic and personal journeys.

What we do

We organize cultural celebrations, social gatherings, and collaborative events that connect Malaysian students with one another and with the wider UW community. From festive potlucks to community outreach, we create spaces where friendships flourish and culture thrives.

Mission

Our mission is to build a strong sense of belonging for Malaysian students at UW by fostering inclusive connections, celebrating our roots, and creating a supportive environment for all.

Vision

We envision a vibrant and welcoming Malaysian community at UW, where every member feels valued, included, and empowered to grow both individually and collectively.

Event Highlights on Campus

Deepavali Event

We did a kolam crafting competition in collaboration with Singapore Student Association. The winner got a $50 gift card!

MASA Fundraising

First ever fundraiser by MASA! We sold homemade Iced Lemon Tea and of course the classic Malaysian drink, Teh Tarik, which is essentially milk tea but in Malaysian way.

CNY celebration

Tons of fun activities were held during the event, such as Yee Sang tossing, paper cutting, dumplings, and more.

Raya Potluck

We celebrated Eid Mubarak (Hari Raya) with our beloved members under the cherry blossom! Everyone brought their favourite Malaysian dish during the potluck.

Game Day

We kicked off the fall quarter with a game day to help our members on getting to know each other!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MASA?

MASA stands for Malaysian Student Association at UW. We build community for Malaysians and friends of Malaysia in Seattle, WA.

Who can join MASA?

Anyone! Everyone at UW is welcomed to join us, whether you’re Malaysian or just interested in the culture!

How do I stay updated on events?

Follow us on Instagram and check this site for the latest updates and announcements.

I have more questions, who can I contact?
