Welcome to MASA!

Malaysian Student Association at University of Washington

Who we are

We are a close-knit group of Malaysian students at the University of Washington, Seattle, brought together by a shared heritage, culture, and a passion for supporting one another in our academic and personal journeys.

What we do

We organize cultural celebrations, social gatherings, and collaborative events that connect Malaysian students with one another and with the wider UW community. From festive potlucks to community outreach, we create spaces where friendships flourish and culture thrives.

Mission

Our mission is to build a strong sense of belonging for Malaysian students at UW by fostering inclusive connections, celebrating our roots, and creating a supportive environment for all.

Vision

We envision a vibrant and welcoming Malaysian community at UW, where every member feels valued, included, and empowered to grow both individually and collectively.
